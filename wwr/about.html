Splash White Water Rafting

Company Purpose:
To deliver thrilling and safe white water rafting adventures that connect people with nature and unforgettable memories.
Mission Statement:
We create world-class rafting experiences that inspire excitement, teamwork, and a love for the outdoors.
Motto:
“Feel the Rush. Ride the Splash.”
Creed:
At Splash, we believe in safety, adventure, and the power of shared moments on the river. Every trip is more than a ride — it’s a journey.

History

Founded in the early 2000s by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts, Splash Water Rafting began with a single raft and a big dream — to share the thrill of white water with adventurers of all skill levels. Starting out on LA’s iconic rivers, we quickly became known for our commitment to safety, fun, and unforgettable river experiences. Over the years, we’ve grown into one of the US’s leading rafting providers, welcoming thousands of guests each year for guided adventures across some of the most scenic and exciting waterways. Our journey is powered by a love for the outdoors and the joy of creating lasting memories on the water.
